Key Insights: The Story in the Data

The "Tourist Gaze"

The overwhelming majority of comments are Neutral. The chat acts as a collective pair of eyes, describing the scene rather than politicizing it.

Positivity Wins

Positive sentiment (~30%) consistently outweighs Negative (~11%). Cultural curiosity beats toxicity in this sample.

Global Context

Frequent mention of "Korea" and "World" shows viewers are constantly comparing Asian urban environments.

09

Limitations & Future Work

The Challenges

  • Sarcasm & Slang: VADER struggles with Gen-Z stream speak (e.g., "Speed is cooking" = Good).
  • Timestamp Gaps: We couldn't map sentiment to specific moments in the video.

The Future

  • Temporal Analysis: Mapping sentiment spikes to real-time events (e.g., Speed eating street food).
  • Multilingual Support: Analyzing Chinese comments alongside English ones.
